Know Our Medical Professionals

Discover and explore the range of medical professionals that we have to meet your healthcare needs.

Tanjiro Kamado

Tuli Services Specialist

Dr. Tanjiro is a licensed medical practitioner specializing in minor surgical procedures, including circumcision services. He completed his medical degree at an accredited medical university and has undergone specialized training in sterile surgical techniques and patient safety protocols. With several years of hands-on clinical experience, Dr. Tanjiro is known for his calm approach, precision, and commitment to patient comfort. He ensures that every procedure is performed in a clean, controlled environment while prioritizing safety, hygiene, and post-procedure care.

Zenitsu Agatsuma

Doctor of Dental Medicine

Dr. Zenitsu is a licensed dentist who earned his Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) degree from a recognized dental institution. He specializes in preventive dentistry, routine check-ups, and basic restorative procedures to promote long-term oral health. Dedicated to patient education, Dr. Zenitsu focuses on helping patients understand proper dental hygiene practices. His gentle approach and attention to detail make dental visits comfortable for patients of all ages.

Maki Zenin

Pediatrician

Dr. Maki is a board-trained pediatrician with extensive experience in providing healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. She completed her medical degree and pediatric residency at a reputable medical center, where she developed a strong foundation in child health and development. Her practice focuses on preventive care, growth monitoring, and early diagnosis of childhood illnesses. Dr. Maki is passionate about creating a friendly and reassuring environment for both children and parents.

Inosuke Hashibira

Registered Nurse

Nurse Inosuke is a registered nurse with professional training in general health assessments and patient monitoring. She graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Nursing and has experience assisting physicians during routine check-ups and clinical procedures. Known for her attentive and compassionate care, Nurse Inosuke plays a key role in conducting vital sign assessments, preparing patients for consultations, and ensuring smooth clinic operations.

Yuta Okkotsu

Immunization & Vaccination Nurse

Nurse Yuta specializes in vaccination and immunization services. She holds a nursing degree and has received additional training in vaccine administration, storage, and patient safety guidelines. She is committed to educating patients and families about the importance of immunization while ensuring that vaccines are administered safely and comfortably. Her calm and reassuring demeanor helps patients feel at ease during vaccination procedures.

Nobara Kugisake

Obstetrician–Gynecologist

Dr. Nobara Kugisake is a licensed gynecologist specializing in women’s reproductive health. She completed her medical degree and obstetrics–gynecology residency at an accredited medical institution. Her expertise includes routine gynecological examinations, reproductive health counseling, and preventive care for women of all ages. Dr. Kugisake is dedicated to providing respectful, confidential, and patient-centered care in a supportive clinical environment.
